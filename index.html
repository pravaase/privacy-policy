Privacy Policy

Pinpoint X - Ultra Location Sharing

Information We Collect

Location Data

Pinpoint X collects your precise location data only when you explicitly request it by tapping the "Get My Location" button. This data includes:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Timestamp of when the location was captured
  • Location accuracy information

App Preferences

We store your app preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Preferred map application (Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze)
  • App settings and configurations

How We Use Your Information

Your location data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your current coordinates within the app
  • Generating shareable map links to your chosen map service
  • Providing location-based functionality as requested by you
Important: We do not store your location data on our servers. All location information remains on your device and is only shared when you explicitly choose to share it.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Pinpoint X does not share your personal information or location data with third parties, except:

  • When you explicitly choose to share your location through the app's sharing features
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Location data is processed locally on your device
  • No persistent storage of location data on external servers
  • Secure transmission protocols when sharing location links
  • Regular security updates and improvements

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your current location data within the app
  • Control: You can choose when to share your location and with whom
  • Deletion: You can clear app data through your device settings
  • Permissions: You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings

Third-Party Services

Pinpoint X integrates with third-party map services:

  • Google Maps: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Waze: Subject to Waze's Privacy Policy

When you share your location through these services, their respective privacy policies apply.

Children's Privacy

Pinpoint X is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please refer to the app's support section or contact us through the official app channels.
